HOW TO PLAY

You will see a text box on the right. You have to fill it before you play.


If you want to play by yourself, fill your name in First Player field, and leave the Second Player field empty
If you fill both or leave both empty, it will be considered as 2 players.
Default names are Player 1 and Player 2.
Also, fill the Target Score field. Fill it with integer between 25-100.
Click Play to play the game, and the game will appear on the left.


As you can see, the first player’s name is in blue. It means that it is player 1’s turn.
This board game contains 3 parts :
- The 5x12 big circles, that is reserved for placing player’s answer.
- The 5x12 small circles, that is reserved for showing the correctness of the answer.
- And 8 different colours and delete button, to choose or remove answer.

GAME

The purpose of this game is to guess the code (5 different colour pegs) in less than 12 trials (the lesser, the better).
Player must click one of the 8 colours provided on the right.
The game start on top of the board.
Once player click the button, it will move the colour into the big circle.
Player can delete the previous answer in each trial. But, once the player has chosen 5 colours
the answer will be marked (cannot be removed anymore).
The mark will be shown like following :
- Black = correct answer and correct position
- White = correct answer and wrong position
- Blank = wrong answer
Once the player achieves 5 black marks, the player win the round.
Points will be received depends on how many trials needed.
- 1 trial = 7 points
- 2 – 4 trials = 5 points
- 5 – 8 trials = 4 points
- 9 – 12 trials = 3 points
- Cannot solve = 0 point.
If the game is 2 players, after each round, it will be another player’s turn.
If any player can achieve at least as written in Target Score, he/she win.
In the end of the game, player will be asked to restart the game (type “Y” or “y”), or not (Type “N” or “n”)
P.S. : Chrome cannot play this game
